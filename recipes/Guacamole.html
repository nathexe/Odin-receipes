GUACAMOLE RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • 1/4 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 jalapeño (optional), finely chopped

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mash the avocados in a bowl.
  2. Add tomato, onion, jalapeño, and lime juice.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Mix and serve with chips or tacos.
Enjoy your guacamole!!
